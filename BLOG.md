# Automate Debt Resolution Workflows with the New Setforth Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node for n8n: the Setforth integration! Built by Velocity BPA, this node brings powerful debt resolution automation capabilities to your n8n workflows.

## What is Setforth?

Setforth (FORTH) is a comprehensive debt resolution platform that combines CRM functionality, payment processing, and e-signature capabilities into one streamlined solution. For businesses managing collections, payment plans, or debt settlement processes, Setforth provides the tools needed to handle sensitive financial workflows professionally and compliantly.

## The Automation Challenge

Debt resolution teams often struggle with manual, repetitive tasks: updating customer records across multiple systems, triggering payment reminders, generating and sending settlement agreements for signature, and reconciling payments. These manual processes are time-consuming, error-prone, and prevent teams from focusing on high-value customer interactions.

## How the Setforth Node Helps

The n8n-nodes-setforth integration bridges the gap between Setforth and your broader business ecosystem. Now you can:

- **Sync customer data** automatically between Setforth and your existing CRM, accounting, or support systems
- **Trigger workflows** based on payment events, agreement status changes, or account updates
- **Automate document generation** and e-signature requests when settlement terms are agreed
- **Create intelligent payment reminders** that adapt based on customer behavior and communication preferences
- **Build comprehensive reporting** by combining Setforth data with other business metrics

## Getting Started

Installing the Setforth node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-setforth


After installation, restart n8n, and you'll find the Setforth node available in your node palette. Configure your API credentials, and you're ready to start building automated workflows.
